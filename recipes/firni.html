Description:

Phirni is a creamy and aromatic Indian dessert made with ground rice, milk, sugar, and fragrant spices like cardamom and saffron. It is distinct from Kheer, which uses whole rice grains and is typically served warm. Phirni, on the other hand, is generally served chilled for a refreshing experience.

Ingridents:

  • Basmati Rice: 2 tbsp (or any other fragrant, non-sticky rice)
  • Sugar: ¼ cup (adjust to your preference)
  • Full Cream Milk: 1 liter
  • Cardamom Powder: ¼ tsp
  • Saffron Milk: 2 tbsp (saffron strands soaked in warm milk)
  • Chopped Dry Fruits: (Almonds, pistachios, etc. for garnish)
  • Water: For soaking rice..
